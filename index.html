Creations:

Cuidadores de Agua Video Game

In 2024 and 2025 I created a curriculum and taught a class to eight students for four months about video game development basics. We went over coding, 3d modeling, and level design. At the end of the teaching portion of the program we tackled completing an educational game related to the EPA Superfund cleanup site in the Lower Duwamish River of Seattle. We cited EPA contacts and resources to create the educational aspects of the game. In the end we created an nine level long game based around the dredging of contaminated sediment in the river. You can find the project files here. And you can find a online version of the game here.
